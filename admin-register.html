Admin Terms and Conditions
Administrator Responsibilities:
  • Maintain confidentiality of all student data
  • Use the system only for authorized educational purposes
  • Monitor attendance accurately and fairly
  • Report any system issues promptly
  • Follow school policies regarding data handling
Security Requirements:
  • Keep your login credentials secure
  • Use strong passwords and change them regularly
  • Log out after each session
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately
Data Protection:

As an administrator, you have access to sensitive student information. You agree to:

  • Comply with all applicable data protection laws
  • Not share student information with unauthorized parties
  • Use data only for legitimate educational purposes
Consequences of Violation:

Violation of these terms may result in:

  • Immediate suspension of admin privileges
  • Reporting to school authorities
  • Legal action if laws are violated
